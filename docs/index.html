Why AnchorDesk

One desk. Every system. Zero lock-in.

Most helpdesks make you choose: their cloud, their workflow, their price per seat. AnchorDesk anchors everything to a database you own and treats every other system as a plug-in.

⚓

Own your service desk

Self-hosted on your Docker or Kubernetes, with PostgreSQL as the single source of truth and a full audit trail on every change. MIT-licensed, no per-seat rent, no data hostage situations — export a printable ticket or the whole database whenever you like.

🔄

Sync both ways, keep the peace

Tickets flow to and from ConnectWise and Jira with conflict handling a human approves. Devices from Tactical RMM, NinjaOne, and Datto merge into one record per machine, so every ticket knows the hardware behind it — and you can run scripts on it without leaving the ticket.

⚡

Move faster with less clicking

Email becomes threaded tickets automatically. SLA clocks, manual deadlines, and automation rules keep promises visible and escalations hands-free. Live updates hit every screen instantly, it all works from a phone, and AI agents connect securely over MCP to work tickets like a technician.

Get started

Up and running before the coffee cools.

One Compose file brings up the database, backend, and web client. Connect your mail and integrations from the admin screen when you're ready.

1
Launch the stack

Docker Compose or Kubernetes manifests, with prebuilt images on GHCR.

2
Connect your tools

Point it at your mailboxes, PSA, and RMMs from Admin — credentials stay on your server.

3
Work the queue

Invite the team with SSO and MFA, set SLA policies, and let automation handle the busywork.

# The whole stack, one command
git clone https://github.com/Spillers-Technology/AnchorDesk
cd AnchorDesk
docker compose up --build
